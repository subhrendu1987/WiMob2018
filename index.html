WiMob 2018

The 14th International Conference on Wireless and Mobile Computing, Networking and Communications (WiMob 2018) is an international forum for the exchange of knowledge and experience among researchers, developers and practitioners of wireless and mobile technology. For fourteen years, the International IEEE WiMob conference has provided unique opportunities for researchers and developers to interact, share new results, show live demonstrations, and discuss emerging directions in Wireless Communications, Mobile Networking and Ubiquitous Computing. WiMob 2018 will be held in Limassol, Cyprus, during 15 - 17 October, 2018. It will be held at St. Raphael Resort Hotel which is located on one of the most renowned and largest beaches in Limassol,.

2018 14th International Conference on Wireless and Mobile Computing, Networking and Communications (WiMob) took place 15-17 October 2018 in Limassol, Cyprus.
